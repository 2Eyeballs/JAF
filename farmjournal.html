Mule Driver

Meet Clementine "Stubborn" Sterling, a seasoned Mule Driver descended from a long line of idealists and cynics. When she’s not cursing at uncooperative pack animals, her hobbies include perfecting the art of kicking herself for questionable life choices that led her into farming.

Sorta Farmer

By day, this southern gentleman negotiates complex contracts and resurrects rusted tractor engines, proving that modern agriculture runs on both grit and ingenuity. When the sun sets, the focus shifts to brewing artisanal cider, smoking pulled pork with collards, and playing bluegrass on the banjo.
