Game of Logging Level 1

Game of Logging (GOL) is the premier chainsaw safety and productivity training program in the country, offering hands-on chainsaw safety training in a competitive environment. 

Top instructors combine demonstration with participation to teach chainsaw safety, productivity, conservation, and cutting techniques.

Get tickets here for our June 20th class

The Class

This is a LEVEL I COURSE. This course focuses on introducing the participant to open-face felling and the development of techniques to safely use it.

Topics covered include personal protective equipment, chainsaw safety features, chainsaw reactive forces, bore cutting, pre-planning the fell, and understanding hinge wood strength.

Requirements

For all GOL classes, participants are expected to bring their own chainsaw with all personal protective equipment (helmet, chaps, leather boots, eye protection, hearing protection). In addition, bring a bagged lunch and water. We will not cancel for weather unless it is severe, so please dress accordingly.
